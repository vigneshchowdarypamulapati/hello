Design Addendum

This version of the page was designed with a light color palette to create a more airy, clean, and modern feel for users. The design choices made are as follows:

  • Color Scheme: The light blue, yellow, and coral red tones create a fresh, vibrant, and friendly environment for the user. These colors were chosen to provide a calm and approachable feeling.
  • Font Choice: 'Space Grotesk' is used to maintain a modern, clean look, ensuring readability while still fitting within the playful and cosmic theme.
  • Interactive Elements: The buttons and inputs now feature a smooth hover effect with transitions that make the interface feel responsive and engaging. The soft blue and red tones on hover reinforce the friendly aesthetic.
  • Responsiveness: The design has been optimized for smaller devices, with the form and button resizing to fit comfortably, ensuring an excellent experience on phones and tablets.
  • Animation: The fade-in animation is kept for a smooth entry effect when the page is loaded, enhancing the user experience by creating a gentle introduction to the content.

Overall, the design shifts towards a light and inviting look that remains fun and interactive, while maintaining ease of use and readability.
